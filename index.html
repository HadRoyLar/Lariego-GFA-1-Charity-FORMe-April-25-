The Right to Repair Association

What is the Repair Association?

Is an association that promotes the ideologies of right to repair. We committed to support individual third-party repair shops by legalizing them as a right. This in turn can help our local econmy by making it more competitive and affordable to repair our electronics.

Why should you support us?

The biggest problem of electronics is their apparent e-waste. Electronic waste is made because of thrown away electronics, because either of its irrepairable state or illegally repairable, unfortunately it is now predicted to reach around 74.7 million metric tons. As an association we propose for the electronics market to make electronics that are easily repairable and also legalizing the repair of it.
